Omega Video Downloader is the ultimate tool for capturing and organizing your favorite media from across the web. Designed with performance and privacy in mind, it provides a seamless experience for downloading videos in high quality and keeping them secure.

Key Features:
- Universal Support: Download from YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, Twitter (X), and many more platforms.
- High Quality: Select your preferred resolution, from data-saving 360p up to stunning 4K.
- Audio Extraction: Convert videos directly to high-quality MP3/audio files.
- Private Locker: Keep your sensitive downloads hidden and secure with a PIN-protected, encrypted locker.
- Modern UI: Experience a premium interface featuring glassmorphism effects, haptic feedback, and buttery-smooth navigation.
- Built-in Player: Watch your downloads immediately with a powerful, gesture-controlled video player.
- Concurrent Downloads: Efficiently download multiple files simultaneously without slowing down.

Omega Video Downloader is open-source and respects your privacy, ensuring all downloads and locker data stay strictly on your device.
