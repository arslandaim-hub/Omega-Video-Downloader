Omega Video Downloader is the ultimate tool for capturing and organizing your favorite media from across the web. Designed with performance and privacy in mind, it provides a seamless experience for downloading videos in high quality and keeping them secure.

Core Functionalities:
- Universal Platform Support: Effortlessly download videos from YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, Twitter (X), and many other popular platforms.
- High-Resolution Downloads: Choose your preferred quality, ranging from mobile-friendly 360p up to breathtaking 4K resolution.
- Integrated Private Locker: Secure your sensitive videos in a PIN-protected, encrypted locker that stays strictly on your device.
- High-Quality Audio Extraction: Convert any video directly into a high-fidelity MP3 or audio file with one tap.
- Advanced Download Manager: Handle multiple concurrent downloads with real-time progress tracking and automated gallery saving.
- Gesture-Controlled Player: Watch your media instantly with a built-in player featuring intuitive brightness, volume, and seek gestures.
- Premium Modern UI: A beautiful "glassmorphism" interface optimized for speed, featuring haptic feedback and buttery-smooth navigation.
- Privacy Focused: No accounts required and no tracking; your data and downloaded content remain entirely under your control.

Omega Video Downloader is open-source and built for users who demand both power and privacy in their media management.
